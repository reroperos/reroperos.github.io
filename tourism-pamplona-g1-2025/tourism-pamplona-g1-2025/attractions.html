Must-Visit Attractions

Explore the best spots in Pamplona Municipality

SAKADA

Nature's Bounty, Served Fresh

Sakáda Cafe Farm is a tranquil, family-owned boutique restaurant and farm stay nestled in the mountains of Pamplona, Negros Oriental, offering a scenic escape with rustic charm and farm-to-table Filipino cuisine.

It is a must-visit destination because of its breathtaking, almost 360-degree panoramic views of the surrounding mountains and coastline, providing a uniquely serene atmosphere perfect for relaxing, enjoying the sunset, and escaping the hustle and bustle of city life.

Location: Situated in the town of Pamplona, approximately 1.5 hours from Dumaguete City

Hours: Open Daily

Highlights: It serves all-day breakfast, lunch, snacks, coffee, and desserts, focusing on Filipino favorites and dishes made with ingredients harvested fresh from their own land (farm-to-table concept).

Cafe Alicia

Your Cozy Coffee Haven

CCafé Alicia is a cozy and peaceful café known for its relaxing atmosphere and beautiful mountain scenery. It offers a variety of meals and drinks that visitors enjoy, especially during a chill break or weekend getaway. The place is also popular for its aesthetic surroundings, making it perfect for photos and quiet moments.

It is a great spot to unwind and de-stress because of its cool breeze and nature views. Many people visit to enjoy good food while appreciating the peaceful environment away from the busy town. It’s also a nice place for bonding with friends, family, or a partner.

Location: Barangay Balayagmanok, Pamplona, Negros Oriental.

Hours: 7:00 AM – 9:00 PM

Specialties: Pasta, Frappes, Cakes, Coffee

RAF & NAT'S KAN-ANAN

Authentic Local Filipino Comfort Food

This well-loved eatery serves traditional Filipino meals at affordable prices. Known for large servings and home-style recipes, it’s a go-to spot for groups, students, and families.

From flavorful soups to grilled favorites, it captures the comforting taste of Filipino home cooking.

Location: Barangay Poblacion, Pamplona, Negros Oriental

Hours: 10:00 AM – 8:00 PM

Must-Try: STK, Balbacua, Bulalo, Larang, Native Chicken, etc.
