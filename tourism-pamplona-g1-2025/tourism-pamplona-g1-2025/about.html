About Pamplona

Discover the culture, nature, and people of the highland municipality of Pamplona

Welcome to the Highlands of Pamplona

Pamplona is a peaceful highland municipality in Negros Oriental known for its cool climate, lush mountain landscapes, eco-friendly farms, and warm community spirit. A nature loverâ€™s destination, Pamplona offers a refreshing break from the busy lowlands.

The municipality is home to scenic viewpoints, agricultural villages, cultural heritage, and growing eco-tourism spots that highlight simplicity, sustainability, and authentic rural living.

History & Heritage

Pamplona traces its roots to early settlers who lived along its river valleys and upland plains. The town was officially established during the Spanish period and named after the Spanish city of Pamplona.

The municipality has preserved its traditions through community celebrations, agricultural festivals, and local crafts that continue to reflect the identity and resilience of Pamplonanhons.

Agri-Eco Tourism

Known for eco-friendly farms, organic gardens, and sustainable rural tourism experiences.

Scenic Highlands

Pamplona offers breathtaking viewpoints, cool breezes, and panoramic mountain landscapes.

Culture & Community

A close-knit and friendly community preserving traditions, festivals, and local heritage.
